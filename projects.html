Spring's SHM Measuring Device

The Spring SHM Measuring Device is a physics-based experimental system designed to measure and observe Simple Harmonic Motion (SHM) in a spring-mass setup. The device helps visualize oscillatory motion and determine parameters such as time period and frequency through practical measurement.

The project demonstrates how theoretical concepts of periodic motion can be applied using a physical system, allowing accurate observation of displacement and oscillation behaviour. By converting experimental readings into measurable outputs, the device helps in better understanding motion characteristics of springs under different loads.
